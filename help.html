What is Guaranteed vs Outsourced?

Guaranteed (or offline) games are completely local and stored on this site. They will continue working barring a site shutdown or catastrophic failure. Outsourced (or online) games, rely on another site being online that I do not control and cannot remake if it becomes blocked.

A Game Isn't Working!

First off, if it's under the Outsourced heading, see the paragraph above. If it's a guaranteed game, I either broke something or the server isn't connecting, but in the latter case it should be impossible for you to read this anyways. Otherwise, it could just be a slow loading time, in which case try to just reload or reopen the tab. If the problem persists, please contact us at (insert support link here)
